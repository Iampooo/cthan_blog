Writing is nature's way of letting you know how sloppy your thinking is.^[This quote is often attributed to Guindon, a cartoonist.]

It requires a deliberate pace, one that aligns with the speed of thought. When we write, we are forced to clarify our intentions. Here is a traditional footnote[^1].

[^1]: This traditional footnote should also render as a sidenote.

### The Tools

- A good keyboard
- A quiet room
- Time

```typescript
function write(thoughts) {
  return thoughts.map(t => clarify(t)).join('\n');
}
```
